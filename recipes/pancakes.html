Pancakes

Description:

This classic favourite takes only 30 minutes in total (Prep Time: 10 mins, Cook Time: 20 mins) and can serve up to 4 people! Fluffy and golden, these pancakes are a delicious, sweet treat for breakfast or whenever you can't resist the guilty pleasure!

Ingredients:

  • All-purpose flour (1 cup)
  • Sugar (2 tbsp.)
  • Baking powder (1 tbsp.)
  • Salt (1/2 tsp.)
  • Olive oil (1 tbsp.)
  • Milk (1 cup)
  • Egg (1, large-sized)
  • Melted butter (2 tbsp.)
  • Vanilla extract (1 tsp.)
  • Maple syrup (for serving)

Pancakes

Description:

This classic favourite takes only 30 minutes in total (Prep Time: 10 mins, Cook Time: 20 mins) and can serve up to 4 people! Fluffy and golden, these pancakes are a delicious, sweet treat for breakfast or whenever you can't resist the guilty pleasure!

Ingredients:

  • All-purpose flour (1 cup)
  • Sugar (2 tbsp.)
  • Baking powder (1 tbsp.)
  • Salt (1/2 tsp.)
  • Olive oil (1 tbsp.)
  • Milk (1 cup)
  • Egg (1, large-sized)
  • Melted butter (2 tbsp.)
  • Vanilla extract (1 tsp.)
  • Maple syrup (for serving)

Steps:

  1. First, add the all-purpose flour, sugar, baking powder and salt in a large mixing bowl, and whisk these dry ingredients together.
  2. Next, add the milk, egg, melted butter and vanilla extract in another mixing bowl and whisk these wet ingredients together.
  3. Then, pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients.
  4. Mix the ingredients together until they are combined. (The batter may look slightly lumpy but that is totally normal.)
  5. Heat up a medium sized frying pan with a tablespoon of olive oil.
  6. Then, pour a Â¼ cup of the batter onto the frying pan for each pancake.
  7. Flip them and cook until they are golden brown. (Normally takes around 2-3 minutes.)
  8. Finally, serve the pancakes warm and generously drizzle maple syrup over the top.

Chocolate Milkshake

Description:

This creamy chocolate milkshake takes only 10 minutes to prepare and can serve up to 2 people! Made with smooth chocolate ice cream, chocolate syrup and a generous amount of double cream, this milkshake is the perfect dream for a chocoholic!

Ingredients:

  • Chocolate ice cream (2 big scoops)
  • Milk (2 cups, full fat)
  • Chocolate Syrup (2 tbsp.)
  • Double Cream (1 cup)
  • Whipped cream (for serving)
  • Sprinkles (for serving)

Steps:

  1. First, add the chcolate ice cream, milk, chocolate syrup and double cream into a blender, and blend until smooth.
  2. Next, pour it into a glass tumbler.
  3. Finally, top the chocolate milkshake with a dollop of whipped cream and a generous pinch of sprinkles before serving.
